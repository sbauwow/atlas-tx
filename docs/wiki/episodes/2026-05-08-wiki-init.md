# 2026-05-08 — Wiki initialization

## Question

Stand up an LLM-maintained, Karpathy-style reference wiki at `docs/wiki/` covering opendata / Texas state agencies / GIS / EPA programs that Atlas TX touches. Scope confirmed as TX state + EPA + adjacent federal (Census, USGS, NOAA, FEMA per their relevance to the current registry).

## Findings

- Schema can be ported almost verbatim from `~/trading-wiki/CLAUDE.md`. Domain retuning needed: most pages decay slowly (govt schemas don't churn weekly), and the relationship vocabulary needs `published_by`, `lives_on`, `derives_from` to express agency / portal / score-input lineage.
- Authoritative truth for registered datasets is [`docs/contracts/dataset-registry.md`](../../contracts/dataset-registry.md). Wiki pages must mirror, not contradict, the registry. Wiki adds context, lineage, gotchas — registry holds canonical IDs and field contracts.
- Existing research notes under [`docs/research/`](../../research/) are the natural raw-source layer; wiki cites them rather than re-pasting.
- AGENTS.md no-stomp protocol: claimed STATE.md row, branched `docs/wiki-init` from `origin/main` in a worktree at `~/atlas-tx-wiki/` to keep the dirty primary worktree untouched.

## Lessons (candidates for future promotion)

- **Wiki contract-deference.** When a wiki page disagrees with a `docs/contracts/` doc, the contract wins. *(Already encoded in CLAUDE.md.)*
- **Bbox-only TWDB snapshot.** The current TWDB hydrology snapshot is bounding-box-only; full polygon export is deferred. Pages that talk about hydrology spatial joins must say "approximate, centroid × bbox" until that changes. *(Encoded in `agencies/twdb.md`.)*
- **EJScreen mode-pinning.** State-relative vs national EJScreen percentiles answer different questions; product surfaces must pin one. *(Encoded in `agencies/epa.md`, `datasets/epa-ejscreen-2024.md`.)*

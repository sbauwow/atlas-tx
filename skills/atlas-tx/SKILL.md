# Atlas TX

Use when an agent needs to:
- discover approved Atlas TX datasets
- compare Texas counties
- summarize environment, social strain, and fiscal context
- cite public Texas data safely

## Guardrails
- Do not claim medical diagnosis or causation.
- Do not give investment advice on municipal debt.
- Always surface source datasets and caveats.
- Treat debt and fiscal outputs as decision-support, not definitive financial truth.
